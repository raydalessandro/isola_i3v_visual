### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se rende troppo "villaggio caratteristico colorato":
> CORRECTION: The Case Basse are HUMBLE working houses, NOT a
> colorful tourist village. The walls are uniformly ochre/sandy
> hand-applied plaster, NOT painted bright colors. Doors are
> weathered natural wood, NOT painted blue/red/green. The cluster
> reads as "fishermen's working houses", lived-in but not
> picturesque-pretty.

#### Se mancano le reti da pesca:
> CORRECTION: FISHING NETS must be visible — spread on poles to
> dry, or stretched between houses, or piled on the shore. They
> are the visual signature of Case Basse Pescatori. Without them
> the houses don't read as fisher dwellings.

#### Se rende le case troppo grandi/uniformi:
> CORRECTION: The houses are SMALL and SLIGHTLY VARIED — single-
> story, irregularly placed, some larger and some smaller, but all
> modest. NOT a uniform row of identical buildings. The variety
> shows they were built one by one over time by working families.
