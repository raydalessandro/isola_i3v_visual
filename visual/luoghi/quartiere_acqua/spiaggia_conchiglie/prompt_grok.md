### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se rende troppo turistico/tropicale:
> CORRECTION: This is a HUMBLE WORKING COAST in a Mediterranean-
> northern style, NOT a tropical beach. The sand is NOT white-coral,
> it's grey-cream weathered. There are NO palm trees, NO beach
> chairs, NO umbrellas. The shells are small and scattered, not
> arranged decoratively. The mood is quiet and lived-in.

#### Se le conchiglie non si vedono:
> CORRECTION: SCATTERED SHELLS must be visible on the sand,
> particularly small CONICAL SPIRAL SHELLS (like a small turritella
> or auger) in soft greys and dark blues. They are sparse but
> visible enough to read as "this is the Spiaggia delle Conchiglie".
> Also some larger smooth beach stones at the high-tide line.
