### ⭐ PROMPT

```
A painterly illustrated landscape detail in the style of Beatrix Potter
and Brian Wildsmith — watercolor + thin sepia ink, warm earthy palette.
NO PEOPLE. NO text.

Scene: GRUNTO'S CAVE — a SHALLOW ROCK ALCOVE under a natural stone
spur in the Burrone (high-altitude rocky ravine). The dwelling place
of an old ibex who chose solitude.

THE CAVE (focal element):
- A SHALLOW ALCOVE in the rocky wall, NOT a deep cave. The opening
  is wide (3-4 meters across) and the recess goes back only ~2-3
  meters. We can see clearly INTO the entire alcove from outside.
- The OVERHANG above: a natural rocky spur projecting from the cliff
  wall, forming a shelter from rain/wind.
- The "floor" of the alcove: a flat rocky surface, partly worn smooth
  in one spot — the place where Grunto sleeps/rests. Possibly some
  dried grass tucked in a corner as bedding (very minimal — Grunto
  is austere).
- INSIDE the alcove (modest, sparse signs of habitation):
  * A small flat stone that serves as Grunto's resting place
  * A few worn ibex tracks in the dust
  * Possibly one or two fragments of old pottery or a worn wooden
    bowl (artifacts of long-ago habitation, signs of generations)
  * NO modern objects, NO furniture, NO decoration

CONTEXT (around the cave):
- The rocky wall of the Burrone visible on either side of the alcove
- The opposite wall of the ravine partly visible (suggesting the
  narrow gorge)
- Possibly a sliver of sky above
- A few hardy plants growing in crevices near the opening (lichen,
  small tufts of grass)

LIGHTING: warm afternoon filtered light reaching the alcove sideways
through the ravine. The interior of the alcove is in soft warm
shadow with reflected light. NEVER darkly mysterious.

ATMOSPHERE: AUSTERE, MINIMAL, LIVED-IN-SPARINGLY. Grunto is a
hermit-ibex, the cave is his chosen solitude. Dignified, never
sinister.

PALETTE: grey-brown rock walls, lichen patches, warm shadow inside
the alcove, soft warm filtered light, dark stone "bed" surface, hint
of dust motes in the light.

NOT INCLUDED:
- NO PEOPLE, NO Grunto visible (just the place)
- NO TEXT
- NO MAGICAL ELEMENTS (no glowing crystals, no fairy lights, no
  mystical fire pit)
- NO MODERN OBJECTS
- NO ELABORATE CAVE PAINTINGS / sigils on walls
- NO FURNITURE (no chairs, no table — Grunto is austere)
- NO BONES / SKULLS / sinister elements
- NO COZY-CABIN feel (the cave is austere, not comfortable)

STYLE: traditional watercolor + thin sepia ink, palette grey-brown
rock + warm filtered light + soft shadow. Beatrix Potter / Wildsmith.
Mood: austere dignity.
```
