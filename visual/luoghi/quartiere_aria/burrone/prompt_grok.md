### ⭐ PROMPT

```
A painterly illustrated landscape scene in the style of Beatrix Potter
and Brian Wildsmith — watercolor + thin sepia ink, warm earthy palette,
hand-drawn children's picture book aesthetic. NO PEOPLE. NO text.

Scene: A NARROW ROCKY RAVINE (Burrone) between the Twin Mountains
(Montagne Gemelle) on a Mediterranean island. High-altitude, austere,
exposed. The dwelling place of Grunto the old ibex.

THE RAVINE:
- A NARROW VALLEY/GORGE between two rocky walls rising steeply on
  either side. The walls are GREY-BROWN granite/limestone, deeply
  textured with cracks and ledges, stained with grey-green lichen
  patches and dark moss in shaded crevices.
- The floor of the ravine is rocky and uneven — scattered fallen
  stones, patches of hardy short grass between the rocks, perhaps a
  thin trickle of water in spring (mostly dry in summer).
- A NATURAL ROCK SPUR (sperone) projecting from one of the walls,
  forming a shallow overhang or alcove — under this overhang is
  GRUNTO'S CAVE (a shallow recess, not a deep cavern).
- The cave is small and humble: just a rocky alcove sheltered by the
  spur, with a flat-stone "floor" inside, possibly a few signs of
  habitation (a worn flat stone where Grunto rests, a few ibex tracks
  in the dust).

CONTEXT:
- The rocky walls of the ravine rise on either side, creating a
  dramatic but not theatrical sense of depth
- IN THE SKY GLIMPSED ABOVE: a strip of sky (blue with high clouds,
  or pale at dawn) visible between the walls
- IN THE BACKGROUND beyond the ravine entrance: hint of higher peaks
  (the Twin Mountains' upper slopes)
- High-altitude flora: hardy small plants in crevices, lichen, no
  trees (too high)

LIGHTING: morning or afternoon depending on scene; soft warm light
falling INTO the ravine from above (since walls are tall, light is
filtered/limited). The cave alcove is in deeper shadow but warmly
lit by reflected light.

ATMOSPHERE: AUSTERE, ELEVATED, ALONE. The Burrone is the high silent
place where Grunto chose to live. Dignified solitude. NEVER
threatening or sinister.

PALETTE: grey-brown rock, lichen green-grey patches, dark crevice
shadows, hardy short grass tufts, soft pale sky strip above, warm
gold filtered light.

NOT INCLUDED:
- NO PEOPLE, NO Grunto visible (just the place, empty)
- NO TEXT
- NO DRAMATIC LIGHTING (no godrays into the cave, no spotlight)
- NO TROPICAL/exotic elements
- NO EVIL/SINISTER atmosphere (no skulls, no dark fog, no wolves)
- NO FAIRY-TALE cave with crystals/glowing stones
- NO CAVE TOO DEEP (it's a SHALLOW alcove under the spur, not a
  deep cavern)
- NO MODERN ELEMENTS

STYLE: traditional watercolor + thin sepia ink, palette grey-brown
rock + lichen + cool shadows + warm filtered sky light. Beatrix
Potter / Brian Wildsmith. Mood: austere, elevated, dignified.
```

### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se la grotta è troppo profonda:
> CORRECTION: Grunto's cave is a SHALLOW ALCOVE under a rock spur,
> NOT a deep cavern. Just a sheltered overhang where the wall
> recedes a few meters, providing protection from rain/wind. We
> should see INTO the alcove from the ravine — it's not dark
> mysterious depths.

#### Se rende sinistro/threatening:
> CORRECTION: The Burrone is AUSTERE but NOT SINISTER. NO dark fog,
> NO ominous shadows, NO danger atmosphere. Grunto chose this place
> for solitude, not as menacing exile. The mood is dignified-alone,
> not threatening.

#### Se rende fairy-tale:
> CORRECTION: This is a REAL ROCKY RAVINE in a Mediterranean island,
> NOT a fantasy cave with magical crystals or glowing stones. Just
> grey-brown rock, lichen, hardy plants. Realistic.
