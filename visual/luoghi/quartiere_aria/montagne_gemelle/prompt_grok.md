### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se rende troppo alpino:
> CORRECTION: The Twin Mountains are MODEST (600-800m), NOT Alps.
> NO glaciers, NO permanent snow caps, NO sheer rock walls of
> Himalayan scale. They are gentle Mediterranean mountains with
> grey-brown rocky slopes and green vegetation on the lower flanks.

#### Se non si vede il Burrone tra i picchi:
> CORRECTION: A NARROW V-SHAPED NOTCH must be visible between the
> two peaks — this is the canonical Burrone (where Grunto lives).
> Without the gap between them, they don't read as "twin mountains
> separated by a ravine".

#### Se manca la nebbia (per scena s01):
> If generating for s01 canonical opening: add MORNING MIST covering
> the lower slopes and partly the Burrone notch, with the peaks
> emerging clean above the mist line. Canonical "Nebbia delle
> Montagne Gemelle".
