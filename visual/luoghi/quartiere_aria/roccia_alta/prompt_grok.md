### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se rende epico/fantasy:
> CORRECTION: This is a HUMBLE PANORAMIC VIEW, not an epic fantasy
> summit. NO eagles flying past, NO godrays through clouds, NO
> sunburst, NO magical glow. The view is wide but quiet. The
> island is modest in scale (~5-10 km across). Atmosphere is
> peaceful, not melodramatic.

#### Se rende troppo distante/zoom-out:
> CORRECTION: The view shows the WHOLE island visible in middle and
> far distance, but not so far that nothing is recognizable. We
> should be able to clearly identify: the village (with Old Tree),
> the four quartieri's distinguishing features (Forno cluster east,
> Orti rings west, Bocca+sea south), the Fiume meandering. The
> camera is HIGH but not impossibly high.
