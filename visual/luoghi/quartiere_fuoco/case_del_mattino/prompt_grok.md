### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se mancano i signature dei mestieri:
> CRITICAL CORRECTION: Each house needs a SUBTLE signature of its
> trade — anvil for blacksmith, hides on racks for tanner, fruit
> trays for drying shed. These are HUMBLE pre-industrial signs,
> not dominant industrial structures.

#### Se rende troppo "town":
> CORRECTION: Only 3-4 small houses in a CLUSTER, not a town. The
> Case del Mattino are a humble row of working dwellings, not a
> village center. Atmosphere intimate, not bustling.

#### Se manca il Forno sullo sfondo:
> CORRECTION: At the LEFT EDGE / FOREGROUND of the frame, a partial
> hint of the FORNO's corner (ochre wall + chimney) should be
> visible — establishing the canonical relationship: Forno first,
> then Case del Mattino along the path. Without this hint, the
> spatial relationship is lost.
