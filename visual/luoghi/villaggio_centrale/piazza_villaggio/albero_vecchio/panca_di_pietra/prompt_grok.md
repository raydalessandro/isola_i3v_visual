### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se la panca risulta troppo lavorata/scolpita:
> CRITICAL CORRECTION: The Stone Bench is HAND-CUT but PLAIN — never
> sculpted, never carved with figures or patterns. It is a working
> stone block, not a monument. Smooth where worn by use, rough at the
> edges. Like a bench in a working village square, not a museum.

#### Se mette le Vecchie del Mercato:
> CORRECTION: NO PEOPLE on the bench. The reference shows the place
> itself, empty. Bible §4.19: the Vecchie del Mercato are "mai
> individuate per nome" and only appear during the noon market — this
> reference is OFF-market hours, the bench is vacant. The Vecchie are
> composed later in scenes that need them.

#### Se manca il contesto Pozzo-Albero:
> CORRECTION: The bench is part of the canonical TRIAD (Tree-Wellhead-
> Bench) at the center of the Piazza (Bible §8.1). The base of the Old
> Tree must be partially visible on one side, the Pozzo on the other.
> Without this triad context the bench loses its meaning — it is not
> a generic park bench, it is the bench BESIDE THE WELL UNDER THE OLD
> TREE.

#### Se la palette vira a freddo/grigio puro:
> CORRECTION: The stone is WARM grey-beige (limestone / sandstone with
> ochre veins), NOT cold pure grey. The Piazza palette is "terracotta,
> legno caldo, verde Albero Vecchio" (Bible §6) — even the stone reads
> warm. NO cold blue-grey, NO stark monochrome.
