### 📋 Variazioni se Grok sbaglia

#### Se rende il pozzo ornato/medievale:
> CORRECTION: The well is HUMBLE and PRE-INDUSTRIAL — simple
> circular stone wellhead, weathered grey-brown fieldstones, simple
> wooden bucket-and-rope. NO ornate wrought-iron frame, NO carved
> stone decorations, NO medieval-castle styling, NO mosaic tile.
> Just functional, used.

#### Se manca l'Albero Vecchio nel frame:
> CORRECTION: The Old Tree's MASSIVE TRUNK must be partly visible
> in the frame (one side or background). The well is canonically
> "next to the Old Tree" — without the tree's presence, the well
> doesn't read as the Pozzo della Piazza.
