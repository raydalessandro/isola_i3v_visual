# Prompt Grok Imagine — `conchiglia_amo`

**Versione:** 1.0
**Data:** 2026-04-30
**Scopo:** generare l'immagine canonica di riferimento della collana-conchiglia di Amo (cormorano, scogliera della Spiaggia delle Conchiglie).

---

## Prompt principale (English, single shot)

```
A simple necklace consisting of ONE single conical shell (a single sea-snail shell, conical/spiral shape, like a small turritella or auger shell) hanging from a length of natural cord (jute or hemp twine, simple rope, NEVER chain or leather strap). The shell is dark — deep slate-grey to dark blue-black with subtle iridescent sheen at the apex, smooth-textured with the natural spiral grooves visible. The shell is small (the size of a fingertip), conical, with a slightly worn tip. The cord is plain, looped, knotted simply at the top of the shell. The necklace is shown isolated as a still-life laid on a sun-warmed flat stone (cliff stone with subtle salt patina) or a smooth weathered piece of driftwood, against a softly out-of-focus background of cliff-top and sea — deep marine blues, distant horizon, hint of beach sand below.

Lighting: warm sea-side morning or late-afternoon light, the kind that catches the iridescence of the dark shell with subtle blue-violet highlights and contrasts with the cooler blue of the sea behind.

Color palette: dark slate-grey to blue-black shell as the focal element (with subtle iridescent sheen), natural cord in warm pale beige, sea blues, sand tones, hint of seaweed-green. NO bright pastel shells, NO conch-pink, NO white shell, NO jewellery-gold, NO synthetic look.

Style: realistic still-life painting, oil-on-canvas feel, painterly brushwork, slight grain. Reminiscent of a coastal still-life — quiet, materially honest, the kind a fisherman would have. NOT cartoon, NOT 3D render, NOT photoreal hyper-detail, NOT flat illustration.

Composition: centered subject, three-quarter view of the necklace showing the single conical shell clearly and the simple knot. Frame includes hint of cliff stone and sea horizon. Aspect ratio 4:5 (portrait).
```

## Note tecniche per Grok Imagine

- **Modalità:** realistico/painterly. Mai cartoon, mai 3D.
- **Aspect ratio:** 4:5 (verticale).
- **Iterazione:** generare 4-6 varianti, selezionare quella che meglio rispetta:
  1. **Una sola conchiglia** (mai una collana di conchiglie multiple)
  2. **Forma conica/spirale** (turritella o auger, non cardium, non valva)
  3. **Color scura** — dark slate / blue-black (mai pink, mai bianco, mai pastello)
  4. **Spago naturale** (jute/hemp, mai catena, mai pelle)
  5. Aspetto da pescatore (mai gioiello, mai costume)
