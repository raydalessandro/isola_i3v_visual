ART STYLE — fixed for the whole saga "L'Isola dei Tre Venti":
Children's picture book illustration. Hand-drawn ink linework with
a fine, slightly textured pen — visible but soft, not heavy or graphic.

exaggerated lashes.

watercolor wash, no harsh contrasts, no dramatic chiaroscuro.

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar,
NO flat vector, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss,
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic, NO horror, NO grim.
European picture book for ages 4-10.

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Elias

```
CHARACTER — Elias:
A human boy, the middle of three brothers (the only humans on the
island). Bipedal, naturalistic anatomy. Narrative age 5-6 years old.
Body proportions: 1:5 head-to-body ratio (school-age child, slightly
younger proportions than Gabriel). Slim but not gaunt build, agile
hands, legs in proportion. He is the middle of the three brothers:
SHORTER than Gabriel (1.0 GU) but TALLER than Noah (0.65 GU). Elias
= 0.85 GU. Slightly more dynamic posture than Gabriel — tends to
lean toward what he is observing or building.

Skin: light warm tone, slightly tanned by island wind and sun. A few
small freckles across the bridge of his nose (signature minor feature,
"warm tones" palette accent).

Hair: short straight hair, lighter brown than Gabriel — light chestnut
to medium-honey brown, natural fall, slightly more tousled than
Gabriel's (Elias bends, picks up, ties — his hair shows it). Never
curly, never long. No bangs over the eyes.

Eyes: warm medium-brown / honey-chestnut, lively and quick, often
looking at something his hands are about to touch. Eyebrows mobile and
expressive (more than Gabriel, less than Noah). Mouth tends to a soft
neutral with one corner that lifts slightly when he has a solution in
mind — his SIGNATURE small lateral SMILE (the "I figured it out" smile).

Hands: school-age child hands, ALWAYS IN MOTION OR HAVING JUST DONE
SOMETHING. Slightly more worked-looking than Gabriel's: a bit of dirt
under the nails, minor scrapes on the knuckles. Elias's hands are his
TOOL — they tie, gather, point, pass.

Clothing (canonical, always present, never changes):
- A WARM ORANGE wool/linen shirt or jumper (terracotta-pumpkin, burnt
  ochre), plain straight cut, sleeves ALWAYS ROLLED UP to mid-forearm
  or higher (even in winter — Elias rolls because he works), small
  round neckline. Comfortable, slightly larger than his size (hand-me-
  down or growing-into).
- Earth-brown / muted ochre rough cloth trousers (similar to Gabriel's
  but a touch lighter), knee-length in summer, calf-length in winter,
  with a simple cord at the waist. Deep side pockets full of small
  things (rope ends, interesting pebbles, things gathered by Noah).
- A small MUSTARD-YELLOW / OCHRE-YELLOW cord or narrow bandana at the
  neck — SIGNATURE VISUAL of Elias. Not a wide scarf — a practical
  narrow cord/bandana that doesn't get in the way of his hands. The
  yellow is his palette accent (saga §6: arancione + giallo).
- Light brown leather low shoes with simple laces, often dusty, laces
  tend to come undone and be re-tied quickly. In summer: leather
  sandals. NEVER barefoot outdoors.
- Seasonal: winter add a warm-brown or ochre-orange wool cloak/cape
  over the base outfit. Spring/summer/autumn: base outfit alone or
  with a dark-pumpkin wool gilet in late autumn.

Hair/clothes state: VISIBLY MORE LIVED-IN than Gabriel — a smudge of
earth on the knees, dust on the elbows where he leans, a small sap or
plant stain on a sleeve. NEVER torn or filthy, NEVER pristine. He is
the brother who passes through things while doing them.

Color palette of Elias: warm orange (terracotta-pumpkin), mustard
yellow (cord/bandana accent — color signature), warm earth brown
(trousers), light leather brown (shoes), warm light skin with
freckles, light chestnut hair, warm honey-brown eyes.

Three behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Builder mode" (default, ~60%): posture leans slightly forward toward
  what he is doing, hands engaged (tying, gathering, pointing), gaze
  focused on the object in his hands. Mouth slightly open in
  concentration, eyebrows a bit drawn together. Elias does NOT stand
  still and watch like Gabriel — Elias WATCHES WHILE DOING.
- "Bridge mode" (relational, ~30%): posture more open, one hand often
  reaching toward one of his brothers (passing something, pointing,
  inviting). Gaze alternates between Gabriel and Noah. Signature small
  lateral smile — "I have an idea". Voice in proposal mode ("We
  could...", "What if...").
- "Still-having-understood mode" (rare, ~10%, S6/S11): posture
  unusually motionless for Elias, hands quieting down, gaze direct on
  something. Silent density, opposite his mobile default. The mode in
  which he receives a silent recognition (s11: the Old Women of the
  Market point at him, he says "Va bene." and stops there).

NEVER: NO huge anime eyes. NO long lashes. NO disney-cute exaggeration.
NO heroic pose, NO sage child wisdom expression. NO middle-child
melancholy victim look (his fear is silent, never displayed). NO
theatrical sibling hug. NO crying. NO wide-open laughing mouth. NO
mascot proportions. NO prince outfit. NO bare feet outdoors. NO flashy
accessories. Always at least one brother in scene EXCEPT for these
canonical reference images.
```
