## STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA (incolla in ogni prompt)

```
STYLE: classic European children's picture book illustration, watercolor and ink linework, hand-painted texture, warm natural light, gentle painterly atmosphere, in the tradition of Beatrix Potter, Brian Wildsmith, Ernest H. Shepard. Soft edges, visible brushstrokes, mild paper grain. Storybook composition, dignified and tender, never cartoonish. Anthropomorphic animal characters with realistic anatomy slightly stylized, expressive but never exaggerated. No outlines hard like vector art. No flat digital colors. No manga, no anime, no 3D render, no Pixar style.
```

## CANONE PERSONAGGIO CARDO (incolla in ogni prompt)

```
CHARACTER CANON — CARDO:
- Species: young wolf cub (lupacchiotto). His backstory and parents are NEVER told (porta socchiusa).
- Anatomy: TALLER than the other cuccioli because of his species — he is the tallest in the cucciolo group. Long lean limbs, always in tension (never relaxed-floppy). Already-thick bushy tail. Erect/pointed ears. The body is built for running — and he is the fastest of all cuccioli.
- Fur palette: grigio-marrone (grey-brown) — EXPLICIT EXCEPTION to the cucciolo §6 rule. Cardo is NOT a paler/softer version of an adult; his coat is grey-brown, slightly variegated, warm-toned but not light. Lighter belly/throat possible.
- Eyes: yellow, lively, slightly provocative — vivaci, un po' provocatori. They glint with mischief, not menace.
- Mouth/teeth: he OFTEN smiles showing his small teeth — "niente di minaccioso, ma un sorriso che mette i compagni un filo all'erta". Provocative, not threatening. The smile is the signature.
- Modalità "provocazione" (default): mid-tease pose — pulling on Bru's tail, making faces at Pun, contradicting Toba just to see how she reacts. Body slightly forward, weight on front paws, tail swishing, smile-with-teeth showing. Energy outward and forward, never aggressive but always one step over the line.
- Modalità "lealtà silenziosa": the rare reverse mode — when a companion is in REAL trouble (not playful), Cardo flips in an instant: sits or stands quietly NEXT TO the companion, no smile, no teasing, just silent presence. Never said, never narrated. Once the trouble passes, he returns to provocation as if nothing happened.
- NEVER:
  * Never in PREDATOR mode — no hunting, no stalking, no fear-of-wolf-as-species, no bared fangs to intimidate. The species is invisible as background tension only, NEVER operative.
  * Never visibly redeeming himself (no apologetic transformation arcs in image).
  * Never "punished" at the end of any scene (no shame poses, no penance).
  * Never truly betraying anyone (the lealtà strana under the provocation is real).
  * Never doing "wolf things" — only cucciolo things, more punzecchio than predator.
  * Never threatening or scary — the smile and teeth are PROVOCATIVE, never menacing.
  * Never adult-shaped or rendered as a small adult wolf.
  * Never modern human clothing.
- ALWAYS:
  * Always the TALLEST of the cuccioli group (signature scale).
  * Always grey-brown fur (signature, NOT the lighter/softer cucciolo palette).
  * Always yellow lively-mischief eyes.
  * Always the bushy tail and erect ears.
  * Always the toothy provocative smile when in provocation mode.
  * Always with limbs in tension (ready to run, never floppy-relaxed).
```

## Note tecniche per Grok Imagine

- Genera ogni immagine con **upload del documento canone Cardo** (questo file) o copia-incolla i blocchi STYLESHEET + CHARACTER CANON in cima a ogni prompt.
- **Sempre un solo aspect ratio per immagine** — non far decidere a Grok, specifica esplicitamente.
- **Re-generation policy:** se la prima generazione vira al menacing/predator (specie operativa), rigenera. Mai accettare: lupo aggressivo, fauci scoperte, posa di caccia, occhi minacciosi.
- **Dopo l'approvazione**, salva con il filename canonico esatto in `visual/personaggi/individuali/cuccioli/cardo/immagini/`.
