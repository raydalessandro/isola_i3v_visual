## CANONE PERSONAGGIO LIÙ (incolla in ogni prompt)

```
CHARACTER CANON — LIÙ:
- Species: tiny young dragonfly (libellulina). Parents not on scene (storia mai raccontata). Attends Stria's school in her own way — flies over, listens from outside, enters now and then.
- Anatomy: TINY — about as long as a child's finger (lunga come un dito di un fratello). Body slim as a twig (un fuscello), elegant. Four transparent wings with iridescent AZZURRO-VERDI (blue-green) reflections — this is her signature palette and the §6 EXCEPTION (she does NOT follow the cucciolo "lighter version of adult" rule). Eyes huge for her size (compound dragonfly eyes, large round, iridescent).
- Palette: body slim grey-green or olive-iridescent, wings transparent with strong blue-green sheen catching light. Eyes dark with iridescent highlights.
- Flight: very fast, then HOVERS perfectly still in mid-air, then darts off in a flash. The stop-in-air pose is a signature visual.
- Sound: when she stops to speak, the wing beat makes a tiny "frrr" — wings visibly humming/blurred at edges.
- Modalità "stop in aria parlando" (default): hovering in mid-air, body horizontal/slightly upright, four wings beating fast (motion-blurred at edges suggesting frrr-hum), large eyes locked on a listener, body small but composed. She is mid-message — entered, saying her line, about to leave.
- Modalità "ascolto da una foglia": perched still on a leaf above adult heads, body folded small, wings folded back, listening to conversations below. She is FORGOTTEN there by the abitanti — invisible to them. She chooses what to repeat and what not. She is NOT a gossip.
- NEVER:
  * Never lingering in a scene for long — she enters, speaks, leaves. Always in motion or about-to-move.
  * Never gossiping out of malice (sceglie cosa ridire — but never with cruelty).
  * Never used as a comic relief in serious scenes — she has lightness but is never a joke character.
  * Never knowing things the narrator has explicitly hidden from the reader — she has limits.
  * Never large or comparable in scale to the other cuccioli — she is the size of a finger.
  * Never grounded heavy/walking — she's almost always flying or perched lightly.
  * Never with cartoon-cute eyes (ojos huge YES, but compound dragonfly eyes, never kawaii anime).
  * Never modern human clothing.
- ALWAYS:
  * Always TINY (signature scale — relative to humans/animals around her, she's finger-length).
  * Always with the canonical blue-green iridescent wings (signature palette §6 exception).
  * Always with the slim-twig body.
  * Always with the over-large compound eyes.
  * Always either hovering, darting, or perched on a leaf.
  * Always with a quality of "passing through" — never settled in a scene.
```

## Note tecniche per Grok Imagine

- Genera ogni immagine con **upload del documento canone Liù** (questo file) o copia-incolla i blocchi STYLESHEET + CHARACTER CANON in cima a ogni prompt.
- **Sempre un solo aspect ratio per immagine** — non far decidere a Grok, specifica esplicitamente.
- **Re-generation policy:** la sfida principale è la SCALA. Liù è grande come un dito di un fratello — se Grok la fa anche solo grande come una mano, è già troppo. Rigenera con riferimenti scala espliciti (foglia, dito, piccola moneta).
- **Dopo l'approvazione**, salva con il filename canonico esatto in `visual/personaggi/individuali/cuccioli/liu/immagini/`.
