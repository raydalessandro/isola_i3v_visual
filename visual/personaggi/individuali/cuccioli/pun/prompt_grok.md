## 🎨 STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA — riusare in OGNI prompt

```
ART STYLE — fixed for the whole saga "L'Isola dei Tre Venti":
Children's picture book illustration. Hand-drawn ink linework with
a fine, slightly textured pen — visible but soft, not heavy or graphic.
Watercolor painting on top of the linework, with gentle washes, soft
gradients, and visible paper grain texture. Earthy natural color palette:
sage greens, warm ochres, terracotta browns, cream and ivory backgrounds,
muted sky blue, soft gray stone tones. Saturation always restrained —
never neon, never glossy, never plastic.

Anthropomorphic animals in the British picture book tradition (Beatrix
Potter, Brian Wildsmith) updated with contemporary warmth and gentle
realism. Characters stand and walk on two legs, wear clothes, have
expressive faces with rounded soft features. Eyes are alive but never
huge anime-style; never disney-cute with exaggerated lashes.
Naturalistic animal anatomy, slightly stylized for warmth, never
cartoon-flat.

Lighting: soft natural light, warm and diffuse, gentle shadows in
watercolor wash, no harsh contrasts, no dramatic chiaroscuro.

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar,
NO flat vector, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss,
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic, NO horror, NO grim.
European picture book for ages 4-10.
```

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Pun

```
CHARACTER — Pun:
A small European hedgehog cub (Erinaceus europaeus, young), son of
Mèmolo. Anthropomorphic, walks on two legs (bipedal stable, but with
the eager skipping gait of a young hedgehog who is more coordinated
than his father). Cub age (equivalent to a human 4-5 years old). Goes
to the school of Stria. Statura: ~0.4 GU on saga scale (small cub,
shorter than Noah 0.65 — about 60% of Noah's height).

Spines: SOFT YOUNG SPINES on the back and crown — short, less
"finished" than Mèmolo's adult spines, slightly fluffier. Pale beige
to light brown with subtle darker tips. The spines lay back, never
bristled.

Body: small, ROUND but slightly more proportioned than Mèmolo (he is
young, not yet full-round-adult). Light cream-beige fur on belly,
face, and underside.

Head and muzzle: long young hedgehog muzzle, slightly shorter than
Mèmolo's adult version, ending in a small black wet nose.

Eyes: SMALL DARK BEADY EYES bright with cub-curiosity. They look
ATTENTIVE in a way that occasionally surprises (Bible §4.13: "memoria
pratica e intuizione laterale"). NOT huge anime, NOT disney-cute
(saga-wide vincolo).

Hands and feet: small four-fingered hedgehog paws, with very small
dark claws. Walks barefoot.

Clothing (canonical, always present, never changes):
- A SMALL CHILD-STYLE TUNIC OR SHIRT in muted ochre / faded warm
  orange (echo of his father Mèmolo's color-register, but slightly
  different shade — Pun is his own person), short sleeves, simple
  cut, reaching mid-thigh. Sometimes a tiny mended patch on the
  elbow (he plays).
- A SMALL CLOTH SCHOOL SATCHEL across his shoulder — woven natural
  fiber, small enough for a young child, with a single closing flap.
  Inside: a small slate-tablet for school, a piece of chalk, a found
  pebble. SIGNATURE VISUAL: the school satchel (canonical of the
  school-cubs).
- BAREFOOT.
- Optional: small light beige short pants in some scenes.

Color palette of Pun: pale beige spines, cream-beige belly fur, small
black eyes, muted ochre tunic (signature color, echo Mèmolo register
but his own), woven brown satchel. Warm-earth register, light tones.

Two behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Eager school-cub" (default ~70%): bright posture, slightly leaned
  forward in the curiosity of "what's next", satchel bouncing, small
  hands gesturing as he speaks. Voice: practical short phrases ("Al
  pozzo, papà." / "Aspetta, lo dico anche a Toba.")
- "Lateral-intuition pause" (rare ~30%): brief moment of stillness
  when he says one of his unexpected sideways observations ("Però se
  gira di là il vento, le foglie cadono di qua."). Posture pauses
  for a second, eyes briefly clear, then back to default eager.

NEVER: NO sapientello "wise little hedgehog" pose (Bible §4.13
vincolo). NO Pun correcting his father with adult tone — he fills a
gap as a cub does. NO Pun copying Mèmolo's confused gestures
(Pun is more coordinated than his father). NO huge anime eyes. NO
spines bristled aggressive. NO sitting on Mèmolo's head. NO modern
backpack — woven satchel. NO eyeglasses on a cub.
```

## ✅ Checklist post-generazione

- [ ] Stile painterly picture book
- [ ] **Riccino europeo cub anatomicamente corretto** (non istrice/porcupine)
- [ ] **Spine giovani soft pale-beige laying back** (non bristled)
- [ ] Pelo cream-beige su faccia, ventre, parte sotto
- [ ] Lungo muso del riccino (cub version, leggermente più corto di Mèmolo)
- [ ] Occhi piccoli neri perlina (non huge anime)
- [ ] **Tunica muted-ochre child-style** (echo Mèmolo register, ma sua propria)
- [ ] **Satchel di stoffa scuola sulla spalla** (FIRMA — controllo critico)
- [ ] A piedi nudi (zampe del riccino)
- [ ] Statura ~0.4 GU (più piccolo di Noah 0.65, più piccolo di Mèmolo 0.55)
- [ ] Modalità di registro corretta:
  - Imm.1: eager school-cub (skip-frenato, mano alza satchel, voce mid-sentence)
  - Imm.2: lateral-intuition pause (fermo, dito laterale, eyes briefly clear)
  - Imm.3: con Mèmolo (Pun più piccolo, mano sul braccio del padre, gesto "io aiuto")
  - Imm.4: 4 vedute neutre
- [ ] Nessun pattern AI bandito (no sapientello, no Disney baby cuteness, no porcupine)
