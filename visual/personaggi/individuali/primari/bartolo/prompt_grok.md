## 🎨 STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA — riusare in OGNI prompt

```
ART STYLE — fixed for the whole saga "L'Isola dei Tre Venti":
Children's picture book illustration. Hand-drawn ink linework with 
a fine, slightly textured pen — visible but soft, not heavy or graphic. 
Watercolor painting on top of the linework, with gentle washes, soft 
gradients, and visible paper grain texture. Earthy natural color palette: 
sage greens, warm ochres, terracotta browns, cream and ivory backgrounds, 
muted sky blue, soft gray stone tones. Saturation always restrained — 
never neon, never glossy, never plastic. 

Anthropomorphic animals in the British picture book tradition (Beatrix 
Potter, Brian Wildsmith) updated with contemporary warmth and gentle 
realism. Characters stand and walk on two legs, wear clothes, have 
expressive faces with rounded soft features. Eyes are alive but never 
huge anime-style; never disney-cute with exaggerated lashes. Naturalistic 
animal anatomy, slightly stylized for warmth, never cartoon-flat. Hands 
and feet of foxes, badgers, hedgehogs etc. retain their natural darker 
fur (black/dark brown "socks") on paws and lower limbs.

Lighting: soft natural light, warm and diffuse, gentle shadows in 
watercolor wash, no harsh contrasts, no dramatic chiaroscuro. The 
overall feeling is of a serene, lived-in world — quiet, dignified, 
slightly nostalgic but contemporary.

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting 
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar, 
NO flat vector illustration, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss, 
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic atmosphere, NO horror, NO grim. 
Aspect of a high-quality contemporary European picture book for ages 4-10.
```

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Bartolo (riusare in TUTTI i prompt che lo includono)

```
CHARACTER — Bartolo:
A very old sea turtle, anthropomorphic, walks on two legs (bipedal posture, 
but stocky, low, slightly leaning forward due to the weight of the shell). 
Very advanced age — the oldest character of the saga, equivalent to a 
human in their 70s or 80s. Stature: a bit taller than a human boy, but 
much wider — the shell adds significant horizontal volume, giving him 
a "planted, immovable" presence.

Build: short compact torso, broad shoulders, full hips, short thick legs. 
The shell rises from the hip area to the base of the neck, covering the 
entire back and partly the sides. Standing posture is slightly inclined 
forward.

Shell: elongated dome shape (sea turtle carapace, flatter than a land 
turtle's). Color: dark like a wet stone — ancient sea-green tending to 
gray-brown, with darker shades toward the edges. Across the entire shell, 
PALE SCRATCHES AND SCARS that cross the scutes — these are old sea scars 
from a lifetime of swimming. Asymmetric, irregular, each with its own 
story. THIS IS HIS SIGNATURE VISUAL FEATURE — must be visible in any 
scene where the back or side of the shell is shown.

Skin: dark green-gray on the back and exposed parts (head, neck, limbs, 
tail), lighter on the belly (pale gray-green). Rough, naturally cracked 
texture — the skin of a very old turtle. Yellowish/cream age spots scattered 
on the sides of the neck and on the cheeks (sign of age).

Head: small in proportion to the body, slightly squared, with a CORNEOUS 
BEAK (curved upper mandible, straight lower) typical of sea turtles. 
NO visible teeth, never shown. The neck skin is leathery, can slightly 
retract into the shell.

Eyes: small, dark (almost black-brown), round, with leathery eyelids 
that close slowly horizontally. SLOW TO CLOSE AND SLOW TO REOPEN — 
between one blink and the next, a whole thought fits. Placid gaze, 
never lively, never hard.

Limbs: heavy, short, robust. Front limbs function as hands — five fingers 
with short claws, slightly webbed (thin membranes between the fingers, 
sea turtle flippers adapted for bipedal use), thick and rough-textured. 
The footprints he leaves while walking are ROUND (heavy weight, no light 
steps). Back limbs similar: short, webbed, plantigrade.

Tail: short, stubby, hanging. Often not visible under the shell when 
standing.

Clothing (always present, never changes):
- A worn loose ROUGH LINEN SHIRT, sand/ivory color, frayed at the sleeve 
  edges, sleeves always rolled up to the elbow, hanging loose at neck 
  and hips, tucked under the shell edge.
- TROUSERS of coarse fabric, slate gray-blue color (washed-out sea water), 
  short to the knee, loose, with a hemp drawstring at the waist.
- BAREFOOT always (even on the boat, even in winter).
- A DARK LEATHER STRAP across the hip with a small fishing knife (practical, 
  not decorative).
- NO shoes, NO hat, NO scarf, NO accessories, NO jewelry, NO captain's hat.

Color palette of Bartolo: ancient sea-green (shell), gray-brown (shell 
edges), dark green-gray (head/neck/limbs), pale green-gray (belly), 
cream-yellow (age spots), pale white-cream (shell scars — signature), 
sand-ivory (shirt), washed gray-blue (trousers), dark brown (leather strap).

State of clothing: everything slightly SALT-CRUSTED — whitish halos on 
the shirt edges, salt traces at the bottom of the trousers. Everything 
is rumpled, never ironed. Hands often show traces of seaweed and humidity. 
Drops of water sometimes on neck and cheeks.

Two behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Still mode" (default, ~80% of scenes): planted posture, hands resting 
  (on the pier railing, on the boat oar, on the knees if seated), eyes 
  half-closed or open and placid, looking at the sea or river — DOES NOT 
  LOOK AT WHO IS SPEAKING. The neck only stretches slowly toward a sound 
  or voice after a moment. Slow time.
- "On the journey mode" (rare, dense — boat scenes): seated at the back 
  of the boat, hands on the oars, BACK STRAIGHT (visual exception, the 
  only time he looks composed), eyes open, looking ahead. Rows with slow 
  implacable regularity. Does not speak while rowing.

NEVER: NO Bartolo running. NO Bartolo angry visibly. NO "wise oriental 
master" with long white beard, robes, staff. NO "old salt pirate" with 
captain hat, hook, pipe, bandana, tattoos. NO "sweet Disney grandpa" 
with big smile, shining eyes, affectionate pose. NO melodramatic sadness. 
NO decorated shell (runes, symbols, paint, carvings). NO Bartolo speaking 
while rowing. NO heroic pose on cliff with cape in wind. NO magical aura. 
NO sparkles on water around him. NO four-legged turtle pose. NO open 
laughter or wide smile. NO visible teeth. NO physical affection 
(hugs, caresses) with the brothers.
```

## 🛠️ Note tecniche per Grok Imagine

- **Modalità consigliata**: illustrazione / acquerello / picture book.
- **Aspect ratio**: come specificato in ciascuna immagine (3:4, 4:5, 16:9).
- **Seed**: salvare il seed dopo la prima generazione canonica riuscita per riuso futuro.
- **Iterazione**: generare 4-6 varianti per ciascuna delle 4 immagini, selezionare la migliore.
- **Coerenza tra le 4 immagini**: usare lo stesso seed (o seed adiacenti) per garantire che Bartolo esca riconoscibile come stesso personaggio in tutte e 4. Se possibile, generare in rapida sequenza nella stessa sessione.

- [ ] **Piedi nudi sempre** (no scarpe, no sandali)
- [ ] Niente cappello, niente accessori decorativi, niente gioielli
