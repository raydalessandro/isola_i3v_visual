## 🎨 STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA — riusare in OGNI prompt

Questo blocco di stile va incollato in ogni prompt di personaggio/luogo/oggetto della saga **L'Isola dei Tre Venti**. Garantisce coerenza visiva tra tutte le illustrazioni.

```
ART STYLE — fixed for the whole saga "L'Isola dei Tre Venti":
Children's picture book illustration. Hand-drawn ink linework with 
a fine, slightly textured pen — visible but soft, not heavy or graphic. 
Watercolor painting on top of the linework, with gentle washes, soft 
gradients, and visible paper grain texture. Earthy natural color palette: 
sage greens, warm ochres, terracotta browns, cream and ivory backgrounds, 
muted sky blue, soft gray stone tones. Saturation always restrained — 
never neon, never glossy, never plastic. 

Anthropomorphic animals in the British picture book tradition (Beatrix 
Potter, Brian Wildsmith) updated with contemporary warmth and gentle 
realism. Characters stand and walk on two legs, wear clothes, have 
expressive faces with rounded soft features. Eyes are alive but never 
huge anime-style; never disney-cute with exaggerated lashes. Naturalistic 
animal anatomy, slightly stylized for warmth, never cartoon-flat.

Lighting: soft natural light, warm and diffuse, gentle shadows in 
watercolor wash, no harsh contrasts, no dramatic chiaroscuro. The 
overall feeling is of a serene, lived-in world — quiet, dignified, 
slightly nostalgic but contemporary.

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting 
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar, 
NO flat vector illustration, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss, 
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic atmosphere, NO horror, NO grim. 
Aspect of a high-quality contemporary European picture book for ages 4-10.
```

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Fiamma (riusare in TUTTI i prompt di Fiamma)

Questo blocco descrive il canone fisso di Fiamma. Va riusato in ogni prompt che la include, in tutte le 12 storie.

```
CHARACTER — Fiamma:
A red fox baker, female, anthropomorphic, walks on two legs (bipedal posture, 
stable). Adult age (equivalent to a human in her late 30s to early 40s). 
Average height — shorter than a tall heron or a stout turtle, taller than 
a hedgehog. Slim but not thin build, soft round belly, narrow shoulders, 
full hips.

Fur color: ember-red on the back (deep brick-red with copper undertones), 
fading to cream-ivory on the belly, throat, and inner thighs. Long bushy 
fox tail held low, with a clean WHITE TIP at the very end (signature 
visual). Triangular pointed ears, alert, with cream interior and thin 
black rims. Pointed muzzle, small black wet nose. Eyes amber-yellow with 
narrow vertical pupils — alive and intelligent, NOT predatory, the look 
of someone who has already understood what you're about to say.

Hands: five soft fingers with black pads and short clean claws, used like 
human hands. Feet: digitigrade fox feet, walks barefoot indoors, wears 
simple low leather sandals outdoors. The hands are ALWAYS DUSTED WITH 
FLOUR.

Clothing (always present, never changes):
- A terracotta-red work apron, rough-woven coarse linen, bib + skirt style 
  with a single large front pocket and waist ties at the back. Length 
  reaches mid-calf. ALWAYS DUSTED WITH FLOUR — uneven dusting, more on 
  the right side and belly. Small scorch marks at the lower hem.
- Underneath: a cream linen shirt with long sleeves rolled up to the 
  elbows, slightly loose at the neck.
- A pale ivory-straw color skirt, simple, no decoration, mid-calf length.
- Hair/head fur: natural, slightly tousled, never combed perfectly. A 
  loose strand often falls on her forehead.
- NO jewelry, NO baker's hat or cap, NO bonnet, NO frills, NO embroidery.

Color palette of Fiamma: ember red, cream ivory, terracotta brown, 
ivory-straw, black accents (nose, eye outline, paw pads), amber yellow eyes.

Three behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Chatter mode" (default in the bakery): relaxed posture, hands kneading 
  or gesturing, soft expression, mouth in conversation, gentle smile, tail 
  swaying slightly.
- "Still mode" (rare, when things turn serious): composed posture, hands 
  crossed over the apron or resting on the table, tail motionless, direct 
  steady gaze, neutral attentive face.
- "Ember mode" (very rare): tense posture, one hand raised or finger 
  pointed, sharp gaze, ears slightly back. NEVER yelling, NEVER angry — 
  composed but cutting.

NEVER: NO sexy/glamour fox pin-up. NO disney cute with huge eyes and 
long lashes. NO matronly affectionate pose with open arms. NO baker's hat. 
NO perfectly clean apron. NO jewelry. NO crying or visible tears. NO 
pure quadruped fox pose.
```

## 🛠️ Note tecniche per Grok Imagine

- **Modalità consigliata**: illustrazione / acquerello / picture book. Evitare modalità "anime", "realistic photo", "3D".
- **Aspect ratio**: come specificato in ciascuna immagine (3:4, 4:5, 16:9).
- **Seed**: salvare il seed dopo la prima generazione canonica per riuso futuro in varianti.
- **Iterazione**: generare 4-6 varianti per ciascuna delle 4 immagini, selezionare la migliore.
- **Coerenza tra le 4 immagini**: usare lo stesso seed (o seed simili) per le 4 immagini per garantire che Fiamma esca riconoscibile come stesso personaggio in tutte e 4.
