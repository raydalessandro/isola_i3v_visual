```

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar,
NO flat vector, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss,
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic, NO horror, NO grim.
```

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Mèmolo

```
CHARACTER — Mèmolo:
A European hedgehog (Erinaceus europaeus) without a fixed trade,
village-center inhabitant, father of Pun. Anthropomorphic, walks on
two legs (bipedal stable, but with a tottering uncertain gait). Adult
age (equivalent to a human in his 60s — "the adult hedgehog who is
no longer afraid of anything"). SMALL AND ROUND, low to the ground.
Statura: ~0.55-0.6 GU on saga scale (smaller than Noah by stature,
NOT by nature — Mèmolo is a SHORT ROUND ADULT, not a child).

Spines: SHORT and SOFT spines on the back and head crown — adult
hedgehog spines, not aggressive looking. The spines lay back rather
than stand up. Pale brown to dark beige with darker tips. They look
SOFT, never threatening. The fur on the belly, face, and underside
is light cream-beige, soft and visible.

Body: small, ROUND build. Round belly, short arms and legs, stubby
limbs. The whole shape reads as a soft round bundle with a long
muzzle.

Head and muzzle: LONG NARROW HEDGEHOG MUZZLE pointing forward, ending
in a small black wet nose. Small dark mouth visible just under the
muzzle tip. The face is light cream-beige fur (not spines).

Eyes: SMALL BLACK BEADY EYES — "blacker and more attentive than you
would say from how he walks" (Bible §4.7). The eyes catch a tiny
highlight. NEVER huge anime, NEVER disney-cute. The default expression
is gently confused, but the eyes themselves are SHARPLY ATTENTIVE —
this is the canonical paradox of Mèmolo (looks confused, sees clearly
for one instant per story).

Hands and feet: small four-fingered hedgehog paws (front) and feet
(back), with small dark claws. Walks barefoot.

Distinctive postural feature: TROTTERELLA — short bursts of fast
trotting on stubby legs, then stops and forgets why he started. He
TANGLES IN HIS OWN STEPS when he hurries. The body language is gently
uncertain.

Clothing (canonical, always present, never changes):
- A SMALL SCARF tied around his neck — ALWAYS KNOTTED CROOKEDLY,
  ALWAYS SLIGHTLY OFF-CENTER. He adjusts it a thousand times a day,
  it never stays straight. SIGNATURE VISUAL of Mèmolo. Color:
  muted ochre / faded warm orange / soft russet — a warm-earth
  middle tone, slightly faded, well-used. The scarf is small (small
  hedgehog body), maybe one full wrap with the tail-end hanging on
  the chest. See visual/oggetti/sciarpa_memolo/scheda.md for details.
- NO other garments above the waist — the spines are the canonical
  upper "covering" of the hedgehog.
- Optional small soft cloth waistcoat or short jacket in winter
  scenes (S08 winter or S10/S12 if cold), in muted earth-brown
  hempcloth.
- Optional small loose trousers or short pants in light beige cloth
  for some scenes (or naked-from-waist-down following hedgehog
  natural register — illustrator preference: small pants are safer
  for picture book context).
- BAREFOOT.

Color palette of Mèmolo: pale brown to dark beige spines (back &
crown), light cream-beige fur (face, belly, underside), small black
beady eyes, small black wet nose, muted ochre / russet scarf
(signature accent), soft warm earth-tones overall. NO bright accents
— Mèmolo lives in the WARM-EARTH register.

Three behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Confused trottering" (default ~70%): slightly bowed posture,
  body in short forward trotting motion or just stopped, head tilted
  as if having forgotten something. Mouth slightly open in mid-
  sentence (typical: "Aspetta dove andavo. Andavo da... no. Sì.
  No."). Hands fidgeting with the scarf to re-knot it. Eyes appear
  unfocused but actually small black and bright.
- "Curled" (rare ~10%): rolled into a SOFT BALL shape, spines visible
  on the outside but laying back (not aggressive bristled), the
  scarf still visible at the curl-edge. Brief gesture — when
  something startles or confuses him. Returns to default quickly.
- "Precise instant" (the rare canonical mode, ~10%, once per story
  if he appears): unusually still posture, head NOT tilted but
  straight, the eyes briefly clear and direct, mouth saying ONE
  precise sentence ("La nebbia oggi viene dal basso, non dall'alto."
  / "L'albero ha aspettato che nessuno fosse sotto." / "Le cose che
  si perdono non si perdono. Si nascondono dove non sai cercare.")
  Then immediately back to confused. He doesn't realize he said it.

NEVER: NO sage-old-hedgehog wise gaze (the precision is CASUAL, he
is not wisely-pretending-to-be-confused — Bible §4.7 vincolo). NO
threatening bristled spines (his spines lay back, soft adult
hedgehog). NO huge cute eyes disney-style. NO laughing with mouth
wide open. NO solving plot problems with his precise sentence — he
opens a way, never closes one. NO Pun (his offspring) sitting on
his head or being "tamed" by him. NO pipe, NO walking stick (he
walks unsteadily but uses no aid). NO eyeglasses. NO vest with
buttons or watch chain (no Wind-in-the-Willows toad style).
```
