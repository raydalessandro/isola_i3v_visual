## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Rovo

```
CHARACTER — Rovo:
A European badger (Meles meles) baker — actually NOT a baker — a
forest-edge dweller. Anthropomorphic, walks on two legs (bipedal,
stable), but with a slight forward-leaning posture, low center of
gravity. Adult age (equivalent to a human in his 50s-60s). Stocky,
short, robust build. Statura: ~0.85-0.9 GU on saga scale (shorter
than Bartolo the turtle, taller than Mèmolo the hedgehog).

Fur: dark grayish on the body, with the CANONICAL WHITE BADGER MASK
on the head — a white stripe from the nose, over the forehead, splitting
into two branches that descend on either side of the ears. Two lighter
bands on the cheeks, contrasting with the BLACK FUR AROUND THE EYES
giving him the typical badger "bandit mask" look. Fur is rough,
slightly tousled, never groomed. Belly slightly lighter than the
flanks. Coat tinged with dust and earth from the Forest.

Head: low, broad skull, elongated robust muzzle with a black moist
nose. Small visible teeth (never shown aggressively). Small round ears,
slightly bent from age/use.

Eyes: small, dark (almost-black brown), close-set, slightly sunken
into the muzzle folds. CANONICAL: HE NEVER LOOKS DIRECTLY AT YOU —
Rovo glances sideways, askance, checks with his peripheral vision.
When he looks straight, it's because he's decided something. Eyebrows
furrowed, mouth pulled down. He growls even when not speaking — the
posture says "I'm busy."

Hands and feet: short strong legs with LONG DIGGER CLAWS (canonical
of badger anatomy, always visible on the digits). Hands (forepaws)
used like human hands — grip, dig, gesture with the chin more than
with the finger. Walks BAREFOOT (no shoes — he is an elder Forest
inhabitant, his badger paws with long claws are his tool).

Distinctive anatomical feature: ONE SHOULDER IS A BIT HIGHER than the
other (canonical Bible — derived from a life of digging and carrying).
Visible in walking, subtle when standing still. When he stands still,
HIS GRAY FUR BLENDS WITH THE GROUND — natural badger camouflage.

Clothing (canonical, always present, never changes):
- A BROWN-GRAY BANDANA tied tight on his forehead under the ears.
  Rough wool/hemp fabric, dusty earth-tone, frayed edges with small
  earth/sap stains. SIGNATURE VISUAL — Rovo is never seen without it.
  See visual/oggetti/bandana_rovo/scheda.md for details.
- A short brown-mud / earth-gray rough hemp tunic or shirt, short
  sleeves or rolled up, V-neck open, length to the hip. Always a bit
  dirty — earth, leaves, Forest dust. Never ironed, never elegant.
- Mud-brown / gray-earth coarse cloth trousers, knee-length to ankle,
  wide at the knee for digging, simple cord at the waist. Deep side
  pockets with small tools (digging implements, seeds, a small stone
  knife).
- ALWAYS BAREFOOT — badger paws with long claws as the tool.
- Optional: a dark wooden gnarled walking stick in some scenes (for
  digging, pointing, keeping at a distance).

Color palette of Rovo: brown-gray bandana (signature accent), brown-
mud / gray-earth tunic and trousers, dark gray fur with white badger
mask, black around the eyes, black moist nose, dark long claws, dark
small eyes. NO bright accents — Rovo lives in the EARTH/FOREST
register.

Three behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Grumpy at the margin" (default, ~70%): forward-leaning posture,
  one hand on his hip, the other pointing with a crooked finger or
  holding the stick, sideways gaze. Eyebrows furrowed, mouth pulled
  down. Speaks short dry phrases ("Not this way." / "I'm busy.")
- "Yields but doesn't say so" (~20%): unusually still posture (he
  stops moving), gaze averted, hands quieting. Forehead unwrinkles
  for a second. NEVER EXPLICITLY SMILES — the yielding shows in a
  tiny posture shift. Voice slightly less dry: "Fine. But just once."
- "Mediated care" (~10%, S03/S04/S11_off): default posture but with
  one small detail saying protection — checks Bru with the corner
  of his eye, places the ball on the ground on the Orchards' side
  for the brothers (s03, a step from the margin), pockets something
  without comment. NO HUGS. NO WORDS OF AFFECTION. The gesture IS
  the care. Voice: addressed to Bru without looking at him: "Here."

NEVER: NO friendly badger smile cartoon-style. NO sage-of-the-forest
(long beard, magical staff, profound master gaze). NO threatening
ogre with bared teeth, ringing growl, aggressive posture (his
grumpiness is verbal, dry, not physical). NO hugging Bru, stroking
his head, calling him "little one". NO laughing with open mouth. NO
quadrupedal pose (he is bipedal stable). NO elegant outfit with
hat, vest, flashy accessories. NO Disney/anime exaggerated rage. NO
explaining things to the brothers in long monologues. NO visibly
moved gaze — at most he averts.
```
