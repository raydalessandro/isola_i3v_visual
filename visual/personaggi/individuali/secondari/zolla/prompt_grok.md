## 🎨 STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA — riusare in OGNI prompt

```
ART STYLE — fixed for the whole saga "L'Isola dei Tre Venti":
Children's picture book illustration. Hand-drawn ink linework with
a fine, slightly textured pen — visible but soft, not heavy or graphic.
Watercolor painting on top of the linework, with gentle washes, soft
gradients, and visible paper grain texture. Earthy natural color palette:
sage greens, warm ochres, terracotta browns, cream and ivory backgrounds,
muted sky blue, soft gray stone tones. Saturation always restrained —
never neon, never glossy, never plastic.

Anthropomorphic animals in the British picture book tradition (Beatrix
Potter, Brian Wildsmith) updated with contemporary warmth and gentle
realism. Characters stand and walk on two legs, wear clothes, have
expressive faces with rounded soft features. Eyes are alive but never
huge anime-style; never disney-cute with exaggerated lashes.
Naturalistic animal anatomy, slightly stylized for warmth, never
cartoon-flat.

Lighting: soft natural light, warm and diffuse, gentle shadows in
watercolor wash, no harsh contrasts, no dramatic chiaroscuro.

NEGATIVE: NO 3D render, NO photorealistic detail, NO oil painting
heaviness, NO anime, NO manga, NO chibi, NO disney cartoon, NO pixar,
NO flat vector, NO comic book style, NO airbrush gloss,
NO neon colors, NO dark gothic, NO horror, NO grim.
European picture book for ages 4-10.
```

## 📐 CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Zolla

```
CHARACTER — Zolla:
An OLD GRAY SQUIRREL (Sciurus carolinensis aged) gatherer-of-things-
that-grow. Anthropomorphic, walks on two legs (bipedal stable, but
with the slightly hunched, careful gait of an old squirrel — the
energy of a squirrel slowed to the rhythm of seasons). Adult age,
ELDERLY (equivalent to a human in his 70s). Small, compact, with
the tail still active but the body slower. Statura: ~0.7-0.75 GU
on saga scale (taller than Mèmolo the hedgehog, shorter than the
brothers).

Fur: SOFT GRAY-SILVER on the back and head, slightly LIGHTER WHITE-
GRAY around the muzzle and on the belly (age-related lightening,
canonical of an elder gray squirrel). Some FAINT BROWN UNDERTONES on
the back where the gray meets the belly. Fur is soft, slightly
fluffy, especially on the tail.

Tail: BIG BUSHY SQUIRREL TAIL — the most visible feature. Held
slightly arched over the back in default, occasionally over the head
when alert. The tail is silvery-gray with a slightly lighter under-
side. The fur on the tail is the softest part of him.

Head: small squirrel head, rounded muzzle ending in a small black
nose. Small mouth visible just under the muzzle.

Eyes: SMALL ROUND DARK BROWN EYES (almost black), bright with the
attention of someone who has counted things for sixty winters. NEVER
huge anime, NEVER disney-cute. The gaze is the gaze of "this nut?
no. that one yes." — practical, immediate, slightly amused at his
own knowledge.

Ears: small rounded squirrel ears with a faint tuft (slightly tufted,
older squirrel character).

Hands and feet: small four-fingered hands (fine for picking up
acorns, nuts, berries one at a time), with small dark claws. Hare
of dexterous gripping. Walks on his bare squirrel feet — never shoes
(island elder convention).

Distinctive postural feature: HE OFTEN HAS HIS HANDS HOLDING
SOMETHING — a nut, an acorn, a seed-pod. Often counts under his
breath ("Quattordici raccolte. Quindici se conto questa.") — the
hands are never empty.

Clothing (canonical, always present, never changes):
- A SHORT BROWN-OCHRE WAISTCOAT or VEST (warm earth-brown, slightly
  faded), open in front, falling to mid-belly. Two SMALL FRONT
  POCKETS for tiny things (a lone acorn, a small seed). The vest
  is old, well-worn, with some small mendings.
- A SMALL POUCH or BISACCIA on his hip, woven from natural fibers
  (jute or hemp), filled with the season's gathering — visible nuts,
  seeds, a few small mushrooms peeking out. SIGNATURE VISUAL:
  the pouch is always there. See visual/oggetti/bisaccia_zolla/scheda.md
  for details.
- Underneath the vest: a simple cream-beige short-sleeved shirt or
  no shirt (bare furred chest), illustrator preference — the vest
  alone is canonical, the shirt optional in summer scenes.
- BAREFOOT (squirrel feet visible).
- Optional: small spectacles on the tip of the nose in some scenes
  (when counting carefully — but rare; he does NOT wear glasses
  routinely, and the canonical default has no glasses).
- Seasonal: in winter add a small scarf of soft russet wool around
  the neck.

Color palette of Zolla: silver-gray fur (back, head, tail, with
white-gray muzzle and belly accents), small black eyes, brown-ochre
vest (signature accent — matches the "earth/seasons" register), jute-
brown bisaccia pouch with visible nuts/seeds, cream-beige shirt
optional. Fall/forest register: silvers, browns, ochres, the
occasional warm russet of a stored chestnut visible in his pouch.

Three behavioral modes (visually distinct):
- "Counting at the doorstep" (default ~50%): seated on a low stone
  step outside his cottage at the edge of the Orti, hands holding
  several small items (acorns, seeds), arranging them in rows on
  the ground, counting under his breath. The bisaccia open beside
  him. Voice: "Questo no. Questo qui sì." / "Quattordici raccolte.
  Quindici se conto questa."
- "Gathering in the bushes" (~30%): in the bushes at the edge of the
  Orti or in the Forest, half-hidden in foliage, hands plucking a
  specific berry/seed/nut from a plant. Body bent forward, tail
  arched. Eyes scanning quickly — squirrel default of "many things
  to see at once". Voice: "Adesso, prima che il vento giri."
- "Walking with full pouch" (~20%): full body in motion along a path
  back to his cottage, the bisaccia obviously full and bouncing
  slightly with each step, tail swaying behind. Voice in the rhythm
  of measured walking, not chatty. "Lascia qualcosa per dopo."

NEVER: NO friendly squirrel cartoon (no Cinderella squirrel-friend,
no Disney-cute exaggerated cheek-pouches, no chipmunk style with
huge cheeks). NO huge cute anime eyes — Zolla's eyes are SMALL,
sharp, practical. NO ecological-message lectures (Bible §4.12
vincolo: no nostalgia ambientalista). NO sage-old-master pose with
profound contemplation. NO modern-style satchel — the bisaccia is
hand-woven jute. NO wearing a beret or chef-hat. NO bushy long beard
on a squirrel — he is age-lightened in fur, but no human-style
beard. NO carrying acorns like jewelry — the pouch is functional.
```

## 🖼️ IMMAGINE 1 — Veduta canonica principale (counting at the doorstep)

**Filename suggerito**: `zolla_canonica_v1_fronte.jpg`

```
[STYLESHEET CANONICA SAGA]

[CANONE PERSONAGGIO — Zolla]

SCENE — Canonical view, counting at the doorstep:
Zolla sits on a low stone or wooden step at the threshold of his
small cottage at the edge of the Orti del Cerchio. Three-quarter
view from the front-right. He is hunched gently forward, both hands
holding small acorns/seeds in front of him. A few items are already
lined up on the ground in front of his feet — one beside the other,
careful row. The bisaccia (jute pouch) is open beside him on the
step, more nuts and seeds visible inside.

His face is concentrated on the counting: small dark eyes looking
down at the items, mouth slightly open as if mid-count ("Quattordici
... quindici se conto questa..."), small tufted ears alert. The
silver-gray fur is soft, the white-gray muzzle clearly visible, the
brown-ochre vest open in front showing the cream-beige shirt
underneath. The bushy tail is slightly arched behind him on the
step.

Background: the cottage threshold, suggested in soft watercolor
wash — warm earth-brown wooden door, the edge of the Orti del
Cerchio (filari of plants visible in the distance), a hint of the
Foresta on one side. Late-summer or autumn light tones.

Lighting: warm late-afternoon natural light from the upper-right,
catching the fur and the acorns in his hands with a gentle golden
glow. The shadows pool around the line of arranged items on the
ground.

Composition: from full body (sitting) to slightly above the head
including the tail, three-quarter view. The hands and the line of
counted items are the focal points. Aspect ratio 3:4 (portrait).
```

NO other figures.

Lighting: dappled warm afternoon light filtered through forest
canopy, soft highlights on Zolla's silver fur and on the items in
his pouch. The bush-foliage in soft shadow.

Composition: from feet (slightly hidden by bushes) to slightly
above the head, three-quarter view. The hand reaching into the
bush and the bisaccia are the focal points. Aspect ratio 4:5
